Christopher Stephen Dinkel

To Whom It May Concern 
 
 
An attorney admitted to practice by this Court may request a certificate of good 
standing, which is the only official document this Court issues certifying to an 
attorney's admission and good standing. 
 
 
An attorney's registration status, date of admission and disciplinary history may 
be viewed through the attorney search feature on the website of the Unified Court 
System. 
 
 
New York State does not register attorneys as active or inactive. 
 
 
An attorney may request a disciplinary history letter from the Attorney 
Grievance Committee of the First Judicial Department. 
 
 
Bar examination history is available from the New York State Board of Law 
Examiners. 
 
 
Instructions, forms and links are available on this Court's website.
