NOTICE REGARDING CBOE INTERVENORS’  
AMENDED CERTIFICATE OF INTERESTED PERSONS 
On May 4, 2026, Intervenors Cboe BYX Exchange, Inc., Cboe BZX Ex-
change, Inc, Cboe C2 Exchange, Inc, Cboe EDGA Exchange, Inc, Cboe EDGX 
Exchange, Inc and Cboe Exchange, Inc. filed an Amended CIP stating that The 
Vanguard Group, Inc. no longer owns a 10% or greater interest in Cboe Global 
Markets, Inc. Doc. 46. Petitioners have no reason to question the accuracy of 
that representation, thus they agree with the deletion of The Vanguard Group, 
Inc. with respect to its interest in Cboe Global Markets, Inc. See 11th Cir. R. 
26.1-4.
