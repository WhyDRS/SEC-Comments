counsel, the clerk may refer the matter to the court for possible disciplinary action against 
counsel in lieu of dismissal." Eleventh Circuit IOP 1 to FRAP 26 provides that "Failure to 
timely file required documents . . . may result in possible disciplinary action against counsel as 
described in Addendum Eight."
