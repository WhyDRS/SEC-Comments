CORRECTIVE ACTION 
 
For motions for reconsideration or petitions for rehearing that are not permitted, no corrective 
action is required or permitted. Your filing will not be considered. 
For mistaken filings, to have your document considered, you must file the document in the 
correct court. 
For all other deficiencies, to have your document considered, you must refile the entire 
document after all the deficiencies identified above have been corrected and you must include 
any required items identified above along with the refiled document. No action will be taken if 
you only provide the missing items without refiling your entire document. 
In addition, if the corrected document is refiled out of time (after its due date), it must be 
accompanied by an appropriate motion, i.e., a motion to file out of time, a motion to reinstate 
if the case has been dismissed, and/or a motion to recall the mandate if the mandate has issued.
