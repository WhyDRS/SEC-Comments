- **2026 Revised Funding Model / interim Executed Share Model** — [Securities Exchange Act Release No. 34-105003 (Mar. 16, 2026)](https://www.sec.gov/files/rules/sro/nms/2026/34-105003.pdf).
  - Approved a revised CAT funding model for a two-year interim period.
  - CAT Fees / Historical CAT Assessments may not be billed after March 31, 2028 absent further Commission action.

- **2023 Funding Model / Executed Share Model** — [Securities Exchange Act Release No. 34-98290 (Sept. 6, 2023)](https://www.sec.gov/files/rules/sro/nms/2023/34-98290.pdf).
  - Replaced the Original Funding Model with an executed-equivalent-share-volume model.
  - Created CAT Fees for prospective costs and Historical CAT Assessments for past costs.
  - Later vacated by the Eleventh Circuit.

- **2016 Original Funding Model** — [Securities Exchange Act Release No. 34-79318 (Nov. 15, 2016)](https://www.sec.gov/files/rules/sro/nms/2016/34-79318.pdf).
  - Approved the original CAT NMS Plan, including Article XI's funding model.
  - Used fixed tiered fees based on market share, message traffic, and CAT Reporter comparability.

- **2020 Financial Accountability Amendments** — [Securities Exchange Act Release No. 34-88890 (May 15, 2020)](https://www.sec.gov/files/rules/final/2020/34-88890.pdf).
  - Funding-related, but not a separate replacement funding model.
  - Added financial-accountability provisions limiting recovery of certain costs if implementation milestones were missed.
