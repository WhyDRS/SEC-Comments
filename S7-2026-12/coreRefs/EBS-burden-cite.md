The big caveat is that the SEC’s estimate excludes SRO EBS requests and does not separately count overhead, storage, PII security, personnel management, or processing costs; a commenter objected to that, and the SEC largely treated those as usual/customary or already counted elsewhere.

Back-of-envelope: if you monetized 35,667 industry hours at $100/hour, that is ~$3.57M/year; at $200/hour, ~$7.13M/year. But that is my estimate, not the SEC’s.
